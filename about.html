An independent voice in the UAE built environment.

Netz Energy is a pure-play energy and environmental advisory practice, headquartered in Abu Dhabi and serving owners, developers and operators across the seven Emirates.

— Our mission

Make every building a lever of measurable performance.

Buildings account for nearly 80% of the UAE's electricity consumption. Most of that consumption is structurally addressable — through better controls, better governance, and better engineering decisions.

Our role is narrow and clear: we are advisors, not contractors. We do not sell equipment. We do not install systems. We do not earn commissions on procurement. That structural independence is what allows us to give clients the answer they actually need — not the one a supplier or an EPC needs them to hear.

— Our positioning

The UAE consultancy market is structured around two dominant logics: compliance (certifications, ratings, ESG reporting) and execution (EPC delivery, equipment install). Both are useful — but neither, on its own, makes a building perform better.

Netz Energy sits in the technical advisory space between them. We translate compliance ambitions into engineering reality, and engineering opportunities into investor-grade business cases. Our clients hire us because they want a building that runs better — not just a binder that says it does.

From compliance to performance. From paper to operating outcomes.

— Three principles

Three principles, applied consistently.

01

Independence.

We do not sell equipment, do not install systems, and do not accept commissions from suppliers. Every recommendation we make is built around the asset's interest — and that independence is auditable.

02

Technical rigor.

We work with international references — ISO 50001, EN ISO 52120, ASHRAE Guidelines, IPMVP — and we apply them properly. No rule-of-thumb savings. No vague claims. Numbers are grounded in the asset's own data.

03

Operating reality.

A report that the operations team cannot use is a report that creates no value. We design recommendations to land in the building, in the BMS console, in the operating budget — not on a shelf.

— European standards, UAE delivery

A consultancy bridge between two technical cultures.

— A French parent, a UAE practice

Netz Energy is the UAE arm of a French energy & environmental engineering group.

Behind Netz Energy stands a French engineering group active since the early 2010s on the energy and environmental performance of professional real estate. The group operates four complementary lines in France — strategy and audit, regulatory advisory, technical engineering and energy-investment piloting — with sister companies covering general contracting, technical maintenance and rooftop photovoltaic.

What this means for our UAE clients: every recommendation we make is back-tested against a portfolio of operating real estate that we and our sister teams already manage in Europe. Our protocols, our calibration models, our retro-Cx playbooks and our M&V templates are not theoretical — they have been refined on real assets, real BMS, real operating budgets, for more than a decade.

The UAE practice is staffed locally, run from Abu Dhabi, and adapted to UAE codes, climate and tariff structures — but the engineering DNA is European, and that gap is exactly the value we sell.

— Standards & frameworks

We work where international standards meet UAE delivery.

01

International reference frame.

ISO 50001, ISO 50002, ISO 50006, ISO 50015 · EN ISO 52120 (BACS classes) · ASHRAE Guidelines 0, 1.4, 0.2 · IPMVP · CSRD / TCFD / GRESB methodologies for stakeholder reporting.

02

UAE regulatory landscape.

Estidama Pearl Rating, Mostadam, RSB Dubai, DoE Abu Dhabi, federal energy efficiency programmes, ADDC / AADC / DEWA / SEWA / FEWA grid frameworks for distributed generation.

03

Investor lens.

Many of our clients have international shareholders, lenders or ESG fund investors. Our reports are designed to satisfy both UAE-domestic stakeholders and international scrutiny — without a translation layer.

— Our footprint

Headquartered in Abu Dhabi. Operating across the seven Emirates.

Netz Energy is based in Abu Dhabi — close to the federal energy regulators, the institutional asset owners, and the major industrial corridors. From there, we serve assets and clients across all seven Emirates: Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah.

Our typical engagement covers a single asset or a small portfolio cluster, but we have the bandwidth and methodology to scale to multi-asset programmes when the value justifies it.
