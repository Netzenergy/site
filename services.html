— Value pillars

Four pillars. One outcome: asset value uplift through measurable energy performance.

Each pillar addresses a distinct lever of value for UAE real estate. We sequence them based on the asset's maturity — quick OPEX wins first, then governance, then capital projects — to compound returns and de-risk capex.

Diagnose and recover OPEX hidden in your existing building controls. Independent BACS audits benchmarked against EN ISO 52120 — typical outcome: 10–25% HVAC savings without capex, aligned with UAE federal compliance and ESG reporting.

— Engagement formats

From a 3-week pilot to a multi-year portfolio programme.

Missions are scaled to the question. We start with a focused, low-risk pilot to demonstrate value — then expand to portfolio-level programmes when the business case is proven on the operating budget.

A

Single-asset Flash Audit.

3 to 5 weeks. One building, one focused diagnostic — typically a BACS audit or a retro-Cx pre-screening. The fastest way to put a number on the OPEX upside hidden in operations.

B

Full Advisory Mission.

8 to 14 weeks. Structured engagement on a defined scope — ISO 50001 deployment, full retro-Cx, or PV feasibility — with a clear deliverable, action plan and verification stage.

C

Portfolio Programme.

6 to 18 months. Multi-asset rollout — typically combining BACS audits, retro-Cx and ISO 50001 across a portfolio. Designed for asset managers and operators seeking systematic OPEX gains.

D

Energy Manager-as-a-Service.

Recurring retainer. Netz Energy operates as a fractional, vendor-neutral energy manager — overseeing M&V, monthly OPEX reporting, BACS oversight and continuous improvement on your behalf.
