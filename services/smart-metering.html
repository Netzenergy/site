Why the field layer matters.

Across the UAE assets we audit, the single most common blocker to energy performance is not the absence of a BMS — it is the absence of reliable, granular measurement. Sub-metering coverage is partial, sensors have drifted out of calibration, and whole categories of consumption are invisible. Without trustworthy data at the field layer, retro-commissioning is guesswork, a BACS class upgrade cannot be verified, and an ISO 50001 baseline cannot be defended to a lender or auditor.

To remove that blocker, Netz Energy supplies a focused, vendor-neutral range of field-layer equipment sourced from our European partners — installed, configured and commissioned by our own project managers, on your site.

Advisory stays independent. Equipment supply is kept structurally separate from our advisory work: any hardware we provide is invoiced on a transparent pass-through basis and is never a condition of an advisory engagement. Our partners are Arcontrols (sensors & terminal controls) and Agescom (multimarque BACS integration & smart metering).

What we supply.

  • Smart sub-meters — electricity, water, BTU/thermal energy meters with pulse, Modbus or LoRa output for granular EUI and tenant-level billing.
  • IoT sensors — temperature, humidity, CO2, air quality, pressure, occupancy and differential-pressure sensors, wired or wireless.
  • Terminal actuators & valves — damper and valve actuators, terminal-unit controllers for VAV, fan-coil and AHU loops.
  • Multifunction controllers — programmable controllers for intelligent management across building-automation applications.
  • Connectivity boxes & gateways — protocol bridges and connectivity kits that simplify installation and integrate legacy equipment.
  • Multimarque BACS integration — supervision that unifies heterogeneous equipment under one platform: BACnet, LonWorks, Modbus, KNX, DALI, LoRa, Sigfox, EnOcean.

How we deliver.

1 — Specify

Starting from your audit findings or instrumentation gap, we draw up a vendor-neutral specification — the right meter or sensor in the right place, sized to the measurement you actually need.

2 — Supply

Equipment is sourced from our European partners and delivered to site, with transparent pass-through pricing and full traceability.

3 — Install & commission

Our project managers coordinate installation, configure addressing and protocols, and commission each device against its control loop — hard hat on site, not just on paper.

4 — Connect & verify

Devices are integrated into your supervision platform, data integrity is verified, and the new measurement points feed straight into your retro-Cx, BACS upgrade or ISO 50001 baseline.
